## Axioms of Probability Theory

$P(A)$ denotes the probability that proposition $A$ is true.

- $0 \leq P(A) \leq 1$
- $P(True) = 1$ and $P(Flase) = 0$
- $P(A \vee B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \wedge B)$ ($\vee$ : or, $\wedge$ : and)

Probabilities are nothing else as frequencies.

## Derived equations

- $P(A) = 1 - P(\neg A)$
